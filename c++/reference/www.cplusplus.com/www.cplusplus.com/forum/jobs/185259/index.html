Control Systems Programmer, in Prague, Czech Republic

elibp (1)
We - the Control System Team of ELI Beamlines - are looking for fulltime employees and students.

We're a building a physics facility ("CERN of laser physics") and our team is developing the control system for this. Based on C++, Tango, some Python, Linux, MTCA, qt.. We're looking for people who are good in C++ and have some affinity with hardware.

(Also, devops and machine safety / PLC programmers and someone good with databases). There is lots of exciting challenges, and different tasks - from algorithms to timing to data acquisition.

We are located Prague, Czech Republic, and our working language is English. There is possibility to sponsor visas.

Take a look at http://www.eli-beams.eu/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Control_system_programmer_971.pdf[1]

Please, no PM / apply directly - mention control system team, even if your CV doesn't exactly match the official job description, it will be forwarded to me.
